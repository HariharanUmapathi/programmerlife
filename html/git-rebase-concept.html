The Synchronization Workflow

Syncing is a two-part process. First, you update your fork from the main project (Upstream). Then, you update your local machine from your fork (Origin). Following these steps prevents merge conflicts and keeps your branches clean.

1. Syncing Fork from Upstream

Pull the latest changes from the original project into your fork.
